Detailed Description

Represents a team in the Zappy game.

The Team class manages all aspects of a team including player membership, resource management, level distribution, victory progress, and statistics. It provides comprehensive tracking of team performance and state.

Key features:

  • Player membership management
  • Resource aggregation and tracking
  • Level distribution analysis
  • Victory progress calculation
  • Egg management
  • Performance statistics (deaths, elevations)
  • Visual representation (team colors)

Teams are central to the Zappy gameplay, with victory conditions based on having players reach maximum level and team coordination.
